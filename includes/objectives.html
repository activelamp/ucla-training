This is a workshop

No spectators

  • We're going to do things the "hard way"
  • We're going to do a lot of typing
  • You will not be left behind
  • We will troubleshoot and fix bugs on the spot
  • The result is you reaching fluency faster
  • Read hard way as "enterprise"
  • Don't treat this as just a lecture.
  • If you have questions, raise your hand and lets have a discussion.
  • We are going to get our hands dirty and actually get stuff working on our local machines.

Training is really a discussion

  • I'll post objectives at the beginning of a section
  • Ask questions when they come to you
  • Ask for help when you need it

Breaks!

  • We'll take a break between each section, or every hour, whichever comes first
  • We'll obviously break for lunch
